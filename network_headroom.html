3. FWSS maximum size before saturation — in real terms
Assumptions based on real FWSS data (FOC Observer, April 2026)
Today: 632 datasets · 1.09M pieces · 2.25 TiB · avg ~1,725 pieces/dataset · avg ~3.9 GB/dataset · avg ~2.3 MB/piece.

Key insight: under typical network conditions, FWSS could grow to ~211,000 datasets and ~751 TiB before pushing the base fee up. Even on the worst day observed in the last 30 days (44.9% network utilisation on 2026-03-26), FWSS could still accommodate ~138,000 datasets. The limiting factor is not Filecoin gas — it's storage provider capacity and economics.
Caveat: the "Others" category in the Starboard CSV is volatile and poorly understood — it spiked to 28,000+ Ggas on 2026-03-26, dominating the worst-day scenario. If this category grows systematically, headroom shrinks. Monitor it.
