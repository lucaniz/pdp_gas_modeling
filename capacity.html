1. Network gas headroom — today
Filecoin's gas target is 5B gas per block × ~4.7 blocks/tipset = ~23.5B gas per tipset. When a tipset exceeds this, the base fee rises exponentially (EIP-1559). FWSS proving happens once per dataset per day — its gas is spread across all 2,880 tipsets/day.

3. Dataset piece distribution — why the mean is misleading
The mean is ~1,729 pieces/dataset — but that's driven by two outlier datasets (868k and 144k pieces). The median is much lower. This matters because the logarithmic model means outliers cost less per piece than you'd expect, but they still dominate total gas. Use the distribution selector in the forecast to model realistic scenarios.
